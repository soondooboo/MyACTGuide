Who am I?

I graduated in 2016 with a Bachelor of Science in Petroleum Engineering and
minor in Mathematics from the University of Oklahoma.

Since then, I have been taking the time to come up with a bold plan to take the information that is already out there and condense it effectively, keeping in mind the fact that you are busy (and probably lazy). My sincere hope is that this guide is placed in the hands of students across the country who are looking for something that will help them with their respective goals. This entire process has taken me quite a bit of time and effort, so if you would so feel compelled, feel free to donate to the cause.
At this point, you may be wondering, why make it free? The goal from the ouset wasn't to make money but to create a product that resonates with students. When thinking about what to write, I thought of myself as a high school student, and what would've helped immensely in my own studies what with the tremendous workload from my various extracurricular activities. I thought of how money was hard to come by for my family, and how my mother would work hard to help make my dreams a reality. I'm paying it forward.

Best of luck.
