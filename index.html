Who am I?



I graduated in 2016 with a Bachelor of Science in Petroleum Engineering and minor in Mathematics from the University of Oklahoma. Since then, I have been taking the time to come up with a bold plan: condense information effectively, keeping in mind the fact that you are busy (and probably lazy). When approaching this goal, I thought of my younger self and how money was hard to come by. The cost of education is increasing rapidly, and this is my way of paying it forward. My sincere hope is that in sharing this guide, students all over the country will have access to a product that helps them reach their respective goals. This entire process, from brainstorming to writing the study guide to creating this website, has required tremendous effort. Feel free to donate and support the cause. Every dollar is appreciated.

Best of luck.
